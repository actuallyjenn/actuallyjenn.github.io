Boarding

Pamper your pets with luxury overnight boarding at Austin Pup Culture! Our rooms are designed to make every dog feel at home—you won’t find any chain-link kennels here. Each room is built to simulate a home-like atmosphere with elegant privacy glass doors, tasteful decorations, and a comfy orthopedic bed.

All lodging accomodations include:

  • Spacious and climate controlled indoor room
  • Doggie daycare comes standard with all lodging
  • 3 outdoor elimination breaks daily in addition to daycare
  • Daily monitoring and health checks by our caring and knowledgeable team
  • Lunch and dinner service (Gourmet meals available à la carte)
  • Raised beds with comfy bedding
  • Administering medications if needed
